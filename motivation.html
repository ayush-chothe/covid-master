Motivation for this project

Why we chose COVID as our theme?

We were informed about this project at the commencement of our current semester. Since then all of us explored a majority of topics ranging from entrepreneurship, gaming, IPL 2020, etc. During this time, We came to know about a new virus named "Covid-19" that had affected the lives of masses in China. Soon this virus made its way to other countries and continents, affecting a total of more than 6.06M people with approx. 371K deaths. US, Brazil, Spain, Russia become the worst-hit countries across the globe.
The COVID-19 virus made its way to India sometime in the cessation of January or early February and only took a while for this virus to advance to community spread. As of June 2, 2020, India as approx. 2L positive cases. Click here for live stats.
This was enough to spark the lights inside us. So, We decided on making a website that can provide all the obligatory data about the virus. We went through a cluster of websites to amass the for lifespan, How to protect yourself, and the number of cases.

A brief history of COVID:

The coronavirus outbreak came to light on December 31, 2019, when China informed the World Health Organisation of a cluster of cases of pneumonia of an unknown cause in Wuhan City in Hubei Province. Subsequently, the disease spread to more Provinces in China, and the rest of the world. The WHO has now declared it a pandemic. The virus has been named SARS-CoV-2 and the disease is now called COVID-19.

How did we learn Web Development?

Before the commencement of this semester, All of us had only a minimal knowledge of Web Development and had only a pinch of an idea of its full potential and applications. We were introduced to the Web Technology subject this semester that acted as the necessary catalyst to embark on our journey on thinking like developers and building flabbergasting projects. The subject taught us the fundamentals of Web Development, enriched our thinking skills towards designing our website, and also taught us advanced web development skills.
We would like to extend our sincere gratitude to SRM University AP and Professor Manikandan V. for lending us an opportunity to contribute our part in this pandemic.
